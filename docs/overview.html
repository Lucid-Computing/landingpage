System Architecture

The Lucid platform establishes a multi-party chain of custody. Because Auditors are just standard containers, you can compose any chain to fit your specific governance needs.

Fig 1.0: An example of a "Regulated Industry" chain.
(You define the stakeholders and the order of operations)

How it fits together

01.

Configure Hardware

Establish the Hardware Root of Trust. This TEE acts as a neutral territory where multiple parties can safely operate without trusting each other.

02.

Compose Chain

Stakeholders push standard containers. The example to the left shows a 3-stage chain, but you can build simpler or more complex pipelines.

03.

Verify Agents

The Verifier Service validates the integrity of the entire chain. If any auditor container is tampered with, the chain breaks and the Agent's identity is revoked.
